Abaut Us

The Glory complex was born out of our curiosity and desire to innovate, discover and create. From the desire to experience as much as possible and feel that everything is open, anything is possible. The love of joy and hospitality and the desire to renew and excite have given birth to an experience that takes the world of events to new heights. We have created an innovative, dynamic and exciting event environment that combines a trail of possibilities. Three magnificent event complexes, with different style and atmosphere: event garden, central hall and amphitheater for events and huge performances. Everything is woven into an infinity of types of events, experiences, conferences and thrills. In this unique mosaic are embedded archeological remains of the Byzantine winepress, which was discovered at the site, comparing a mysterious and magical garnet. You are welcome to visit the Glory complex. Stand on the edge and observe the most unique and exciting event environment in Israel. The garden

Entering the gates of the event garden is like entering a garden experience from the legends. Three dunams of spectacular and magical nature are revealed before us. Natural and rich vegetation, elegant waterfalls, streams that flow slowly, are scattered in the garden space as a jewel of nature, and pleasant lighting interspersed with diamonds between the paths of the place. To them is added the melody of the rippling water, the chirping of birds and the quiet nature of the surroundings. The venue is suitable for an event of up to 550 people sitting or 1,400 having a cocktail. The garden complex is covered throughout the year. Its vast spaces allow for interesting combinations and the creation of diverse event corners.

The Temple

The Temple of Events as it is called. Summit and stage for powerful events. The architecture of the place is a sophisticated and innovative design statement. A play between aluminum, metal and cylindrical materials, creating a texture of Modernity alongside roots. The venue hosts events for up to 1,000 seated and 1,600 people for a cocktail. The vast space allows for the production of huge events.
